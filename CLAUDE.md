## Content Style

Tone should be:

* Confident
* Premium
* Clear
* Business-focused
* Not overhyped
* Not generic
* Not filled with buzzwords

Avoid weak lines like:

* “We provide the best solutions”
* “Your trusted partner”
* “Transform your business”

Prefer specific outcome-based copy.

## Development Rules

* Keep components clean and reusable.
* Use TypeScript properly.
* Use Tailwind utility classes.
* Use Framer Motion only where it improves experience.
* Do not over-animate.
* Keep performance high.
* Avoid unnecessary dependencies.
* Ensure mobile responsiveness.
* Ensure `npm run build` passes.
* Do not touch email/DNS/hosting settings.
* Do not remove existing working routes unless replacing properly.
